Lesson 1: Introduction to OpenCV

What is OpenCV?

OpenCV is like eyes for a computer. It helps programs see and understand images and videos. Imagine your drone needs to avoid trees - OpenCV can help it see the trees!

How will we learn?

We will learn by doing! Each lesson gives you small pieces of code. You will learn to make your drone see people, trees, and birds. You will change backgrounds - like putting your office instead of a beach.

What you need

Just repeat the code, memorize the commands, and try to complete the task. This course teaches coding.
Later, we'll add a simulator for visualizing the code on real images.
